✦ About the Event

A Celebration of
Literary Excellence

✦

ELOQUENCE 2026 is an inter-college literary competition organised by the English Literary Club of MIT First Grade College, Mysuru — a grand arena where the power of words takes centre stage.

Bringing together the brightest literary minds from colleges across the region, ELOQUENCE is a platform to showcase creativity, wit, and mastery of English through uniquely crafted competitive rounds. A prestigious tradition of the Department of English.

✦ Competition Rounds

The Four Events

✦

Four meticulously crafted rounds to test vocabulary, wit, precision, and the art of persuasion.

01

Spell Bound

A high-stakes spelling competition challenging participants to spell complex and uncommon words under pressure. Test vocabulary mastery and orthographic precision.

Vocabulary · Spelling
02

Heads Up

A fast-paced word guessing challenge where one partner describes while the other deciphers — testing communication, quick thinking, and literary intelligence.

Team · Quick Wit
03

Bluff Master

The ultimate test of storytelling and persuasion — craft convincing narratives, real or fictional, and challenge others to distinguish truth from magnificent bluff.

Storytelling · Strategy
04

Precis Writing

An exercise in concise, powerful expression — craft impactful written content within a strict word limit, demonstrating clarity and mastery of the language.

Writing · Precision

✦ Guidelines

General Instructions

✦
01

Reporting & Registration: All participants must report to the venue at 9:15 AM and complete registration with the event coordinators before the competition begins.

02

Team Requirement: Each event will be conducted in teams of two participants.

03

Registration Fee: A non-refundable registration fee of ₹250 per team must be paid at the time of registration.

04

Identification: Participants must carry a valid college ID card and present it to the organizers whenever requested.

05

Registration Details: Participants must provide accurate registration details. Ensure the email ID is correct, as the participation certificate will be sent to that address.

06

Discipline & Conduct: Participants must maintain discipline, decorum, and professional behavior and strictly follow all instructions given by the organizers and judges at all times.

07

Time Regulations: The time limit assigned for each round must be strictly followed by all teams.

08

Prohibited Actions: Mobile phones are strictly prohibited during the event. Any form of malpractice, unfair means, or misconduct will result in immediate disqualification.

09

Event Modifications: The organizing committee reserves the right to modify rules, schedules, or add/remove rounds depending on time, participation, or other necessary circumstances.

10

Judges' Decision: The decision of the judges will be final and binding, and all results will be determined solely by the judging panel.
